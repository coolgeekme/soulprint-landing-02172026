# SoulPrint: Your AI Best Friend
### The Simple Version

---

## What Is SoulPrint?

**SoulPrint is an AI that actually knows you.**

Regular AI chatbots (like ChatGPT) forget everything after each conversation. They don't remember your name, your job, your favorite things, or what you talked about yesterday.

SoulPrint is different. It **remembers**.

---

## How Does It Work?

### Step 1: Import Your History
You can upload your old ChatGPT conversations. SoulPrint reads through them and learns:
- How you talk
- What you like
- What you've been working on
- Your personality

### Step 2: It Builds Your "SoulPrint"
Think of it like a fingerprint, but for your personality. SoulPrint creates a profile of who you are based on everything you've shared.

### Step 3: It Remembers Everything
Every time you chat, SoulPrint saves the important stuff. Next time you talk, it remembers.

**Example:**
- Monday: "I have a job interview on Friday"
- Friday: SoulPrint asks "How did your interview go?"

Regular AI can't do this. SoulPrint can.

## Why Text Messages?

Your best friends live in your text messages. That's where real conversations happen.

SoulPrint works through **SMS** (text messages). You save it as a contact on your phone, just like a friend. Then you text it whenever you want.

No app to download. No login. Just text.

## The Secret Sauce: Memory That Works

Most AI has a problem: it can only remember a little bit at a time. Give it too much information and it gets confused.

SoulPrint fixes this with something called **RLM** (Retrieval Long-term Memory).

**How it works:**
1. Everything you say gets saved
2. When you ask a question, SoulPrint searches your history
3. It finds the relevant stuff and uses it to answer

This means SoulPrint can remember **years** of conversations without getting confused.
