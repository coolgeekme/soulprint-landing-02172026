# Feedback + Research Blueprint

## Goal
Collect fast, actionable insights from waitlist users.

## Quick Feedback Loops
- 2–3 question survey after signup
- 7-day follow-up asking for pain points
- Optional short interview scheduling

## Suggested Questions
1) What’s your main use case?
2) What’s your role/company size?
3) What would make you invite 3 others?

## Segmentation
- High intent: replies or CTA clicks
- Region-specific cohorts
- Role-based cohorts

## Tools
- Simple form (Tally/Typeform)
- Streak task: “Invite to interview”

## Implementation Checklist
- [ ] Add post-signup survey link
- [ ] Store survey responses
- [ ] Trigger interview invite for high intent
